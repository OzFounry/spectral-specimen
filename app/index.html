Spectral is a typeface primarily intended for use inside Google’s Docs and Spreadsheet, its apps for everyday editing and contentheavy documents. Designed by Production Type for onscreen performance, Spectral lies in the thin fringe of multipurpose text typefaces that do not fall short in critical reading environments. A straigtforward serif face in 7 weights, Spectral’s ambition is to offer an efficient and beautiful design in a mainstream application.
